Name this term
A major triad built on ra (↓^2), typically found in first inversion. Essentially a chromatic version of iio6 — a chromatic predominant. More dramatic than iio6 due to its chromatic root. The ↓^2 resolves down to ^7, sometimes passing through ^1.
